Sami Plagiarism Checker


Upload Document

Input text, or upload document in Northern Sami to check for plagiarism. The document is pre-processed, cleaned, and serialized.

Vector Transformation

The following document is transformed into vectors using word embeddings from our Sami foundational model. It is a transformer model, using BLOOM architecture.

Similarity Search

Vectors are used to perform a similarity search on our dedicated Sami database, containing a dataset of 22 million tokens.

Retrieve Documents

Relevant documents are retrieved and displayed.
